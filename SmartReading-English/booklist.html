Recommended Book List

Booklists are created by SRE team-developed AI reader-grading technology that can accurately analyze a text's level of difficulty (referred to as their SRE value). The SRE team then creates graded reader booklists for English learners in line with their Taiwanese school grade level. No matter the reading level, every learner can find books that are best suited to their needs.
