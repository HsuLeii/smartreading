About SRE

01

Introduction

SRE (Smart Reading-English) is an online educational platform offering English reading materials to English learners in Taiwan. SRE evaluates a learner's reading abilities through a placement test and utilizes AI techonogy to then provide adaptive reading recommendations best suited for the learner's reading level. Personalized book lists are beneficial for building a learner's motivation and interest in English reading. Upon finishing a book, learners can test their reading comprehension by taking a quiz.

02

Features

SRE is a convenient platform that works in real-time. Learners can select level-appropriate books according to their interests and test their English reading skills at any time, cultivating their reading habits in a natural and effective manner. Learners can also check and improve their reading comprehension skills by taking post-reading quizzes.

03

Applications

In addition to having access to a rich variety of English books, learners can use the SRE platform to build their English reading abilities alongside their global and cultural awareness. The SRE platform also features fun interactive missions for learners to complete, allowing them to enjoy their reading experience while boosting their motivation for learning. Teachers can use the SRE platform to view their student's test scores and precisely evaluate their reading levels in order to design effective class activities. Parents can also utilize the platform to monitor their children's reading level and progress by viewing their learning portfolio. These features can thus assist parents with planning their children's reading goals.
