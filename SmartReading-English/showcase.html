老師

蔡羽涵

公文數學 7A班

Courage

Level 5

SRE：442

完成時間：2025-06-05 11:03:01

創作呈現：

The story of Bella’s adventure to the old castle teaches us an important lesson: courage is not something we get from others—it already lives inside us. At first, Bella was shy and afraid. She thought she needed a fairy to give her courage. But through the journey, she faced challenges like the tiger, the lion, and the dragon. Each time, she and her friend Beth bravely answered questions about fear and helplessness with one powerful word—“courage.” This story reminds me that even when I feel scared, helpless, or sad, I can be strong. I don’t have to wait for someone else to give me courage—I already have it in my heart. I just need to trust myself and keep going, just like Bella did.
